Tom Armstrong

Tom is Associate Professor of Computer Science in the Department of Math and Computer Science at Wheaton College in Norton, Massachusetts. In Spring 2016, he is on sabbatical from Wheaton. He served as Resident Director of the Wheaton in Bhutan program at the Royal Thimphu College. He was a Fulbright-Nehru Scholar at the Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University in Patan, Gujarat, India
